🧬 Ibyerekeye Muganga AI

Muganga AI ni platform ifasha mu gukusanya, kubika no gusesengura amakuru y’ubuzima bw’abantu. Ikoresha Artificial Intelligence kugira ngo igaragaze amakuru y’ingenzi afasha mu gufata ibyemezo by’ubuvuzi bwihuse kandi bufite ishingiro.

  • ✔️ Gufasha mu gutahura hakiri kare indwara zikomeye.
  • ✔️ Kugabanya amakosa y’ubuvuzi binyuze mu isesengura ry’amakuru.
  • ✔️ Gutanga raporo zihuse z’ubuzima bw’umurwayi.

💡 Serivisi Dutanga

Kubika Ibipimo

Tubika amakuru nk’umutima, ubushyuhe, umuvuduko w’amaraso, ibiro n’ibindi bifasha mu gukurikirana ubuzima.

Isesengura ry’Amakuru

System yacu isesengura amakuru kugira ngo hagaragare abakeneweho ubufasha bwihuse n’abari mu byago.

Raporo Zihuse

Abaganga babona raporo zihuse kandi zishingiye ku byavuye mu bipimo byose by’umurwayi.
